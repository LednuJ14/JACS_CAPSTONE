# Problem Survey Results
## JACS Property Management Platform - User Pain Points Analysis

---

## Introduction

This section presents the results of the Problem Survey conducted to identify current challenges and pain points faced by property managers, tenants, and property owners in Cebu City, Philippines, regarding property management and rental processes. The survey aimed to understand the existing problems in property management workflows, identify areas of difficulty, and gather insights on current tools and methods being used.

The survey was distributed to property managers, property owners, landlords, tenants, and real estate agents in Cebu City. The data collected provides valuable insights into the pain points that the JACS Property Management Platform aims to address.
