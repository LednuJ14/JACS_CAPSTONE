# Solution Survey Results

## Introduction

This section presents the results of the Solution Survey conducted to validate proposed features and solutions for the JACS Property Management Platform. The survey aimed to gather feedback on user acceptance, usability preferences, and willingness to adopt the platform. The survey also assessed the perceived value of various features, subscription plan preferences, and overall platform interest.

The survey was distributed to property managers, property owners, landlords, tenants, and real estate agents in Cebu City. The data collected provides valuable insights into feature acceptance, user preferences, and market readiness for the JACS Property Management Platform.
